The goal of this challenge is to predict taxi demand (total # of taxi trips) in downtown Boston based on historical taxi data and other related data sets (weather, events, social media data).

The Contest

We have collected 2 months worth of data (see below for descriptions of the datasets). In this challenge we will give you all of the data except for several days of taxi data that we leave out. Your job is to predict the total number of taxi trips within a number different 2 hour time spans during these omitted days at several locations in downtown Boston.

The locations will be specified as latitude, longitude points, and the prediction will be within a 250 meter radius of those points.

We will provide an initial training dataset for you to construct models, along with an an initial test dataset and set of N locations, specified by lat, lon center coordinates and time, for you to predict the number of taxi trips initiated within the two hour period following that time within a 250 m radius of the center point. You can submit predictions for this dataset as many times as you wish, and we will provide a leaderboard that updates your score on each submission to track contestant progress against each other. The initial test dataset is for validation purposes only and has no effect on the final evaluation.

In the last week of the competition, we will release the final test dataset on which contestants will be ultimately compared. You may submit as many times as you wish but the final scores will be released only at the end of the competition.

Final trial data is not yet available.
