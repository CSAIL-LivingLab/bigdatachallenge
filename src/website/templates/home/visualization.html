The Contest

The Visualization Challenge goal is to visualize the transportation data provided in an informative, beautiful, or surprising way. We are particularly interested in visualizations that contrast or compare taxi transportation ridership in Downtown and relate them to nearby events and locales. Visualizations may include maps, animations, interactive websites or static graphics and ideally should reveal new or interesting trends and patterns. We will have a Judging Panel consisting of visualization experts, data scientists, and representatives from the City of Boston.

The Judging Panel will pick the top 2 visualizations.

The Judging Panel

  • Vineet Gupta, Transportation Planning Director, City of Boston
  • Haris Koutsopoulos, Research Associate and Professor of Transport Science, MIT
  • Carlo Ratti, Senseable City Lab, MIT
  • Mona Vernon, Emerging Technology, Thomson Reuters
  • Martin Wattenberg, Google, Cambridge Lab
